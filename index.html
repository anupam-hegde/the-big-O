Our Mission

THE BIG O is a competitive coding club where passionate programmers come together to sharpen their skills, tackle algorithmic challenges, and compete in national and international coding contests. We believe that the best way to grow as a developer is through consistent practice, peer learning, and healthy competition.

Whether you're just starting your coding journey or you're a seasoned competitive programmer, THE BIG O welcomes you with open arms. Our weekly sessions, workshops, and contests are designed to push your limits and help you achieve your full potential.
